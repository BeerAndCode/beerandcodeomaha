more beer, less code.

Tuesday December 14, 2010 @ 6pm.

Upstream Brewery Legacy - 171th Center

During the month of December, Upstream has one of the best beers in Omaha:  The Ebenezer.  They aren't paying me to say that and since (@andypeters) am organizing this event, I picked Upstream for this reason :).

Upstream has wifi, but it doesn't work well in my experience.  Also I figure with the holidays coming and the cold weather going we could also use more of a "social" time.  I know I'd like to hear about what your working on and what are your goals for next year.  Share with the rest there.  Meet some new people.  Take a moment to learn more about the folks you've seen or followed on twitter in the last few months.

Have you not been to a Beer and Code event?  Be sure to come this time!  Meet someone new.  Learn something new.  Find out why you should come to the next events?

Been to them before?  Bring a friend.  Show them what they are missing.  Introduce them to everyone.  Play pool.  Talk code.  Talk goals.  These are the people that can help; because we want to.  

Every programmer is welcome!!!

We have the small party room upstairs at the Upstream.  Its the room with the darts and the goofy paintings.  The reservation of the room includes one reserved pool table; we can always get more.

Next month/year will commence our normal Beer and Code schedule of events: Bar, Wifi, Programmers, Laptops and Code.

Questions?  Ask us on twitter:  @BeerCodeOmaha
