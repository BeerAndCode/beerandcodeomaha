Join everyone this Wednesday, February 16th.  We are planning a West Omaha event at the Tropics Bar and Grill; just south of 168th and Maple.  I'll be there at 5pm, so come anytime after that. 

The good:

  • They have great WIFI.  Its strong all around the building and so far every time I've been there it works.
  • Really nice owners.  Heck he said, we can bring extension cords, move tables, etc.
  • A great food menu; great burgers.

The bad:

  • Their drink select isn't great.  They have Lucky Bucket, Boulevard, and a few others on tap, but nothing exotic.  Not a ton in bottles either.  Last time we were at Dundee Dell... no where near the selection.
  • The place is big, but there is really one spot in the entire place to plug in.  The owner said I could bring an extension cord and some power strips though.
