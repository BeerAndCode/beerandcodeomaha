I'm very excited to announce that the Omaha Beer && Code group is going to meet at the Lucky Bucket Brewery this month.  They have a tasting room with wifi, tables, and the typical needed room to write/talk code and have a good drink.  I'm told there will be six taps of all the Lucky Bucket brews you can taste.  I know they just released a spring brew, so do try that.

Come early!  The place officially opens at 4pm.  They will be doing tours of the brewery for $3.  I'm planning to make it for a tour.

They close at 7pm, a little earlier than we normally go until.  Don't show up late.

Date: Wednesday April 18, 2012

Place:  Lucky Bucket Brewry (the tasting room).  

Address:  11941 Centennial Road, Suite 1  La Vista, NA 68128 (Google Map)

Time: 5PM - 7PM  (They open at 4pm and will do tours!)
