Mark your calendar for Wednesday December 21, 2011!  We are having another end of the year celebration.  Thats right, you made it this far... time to have a fine beer and chat with fellow programmers!

Last year I sort of threw together off the cuff a end of the year celebration at Upsteam.  A lot of people showed up and it was a good time.  Who knew!?  This year, we're "knocking it up a notch" (Futurama joke).  The party is at Krug Park.  ... Krug Park, was such a great place we wanted to have a bigger party there.

Show up thirsty for beer and good conversations.  Leave the laptops at home this time, as its mostly a social thing (unless you want to bring the laptops to work or show off some great work; this is Beer && Code after all).  

Spread the word to other programmers.  It'd be great to have a great turn out!

I'm still putting together the final details, so watch the website for more details.
