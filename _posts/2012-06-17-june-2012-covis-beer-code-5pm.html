June 2012 is here, and we have a great location lined up for this month's: CoVis Co-Working.  Be there this Wednesday!  

CoVis CoWorking is providing some beers for everyone, but feel free to bring your own as well.  If you remember a few months back we visited CAMP CoWorking, now we are visiting this CoWorking facility.

For those that don't already know, CoVis CoWorking is a new co-working facility located just off i680 and Pacific. It's in the red brick buildings called "Shaker Place" next door to Minute Man Printing and a Massage Facility.  Skid Vis said he'd be buy a couple cases of beers for everyone, but also feel free to bring your own brews!  There is a good sized refrigerator, so everything will stay cool.  Also, there is plenty of parking in the area so don't worry about that.

CoVis CoWorking opens their doors all day free of charge every Wednesday for #CoWorkingWednesday.  Beer && Code officially gets started around 5pm on Wednesday, but feel free to stop by earlier!  You are invited to stop by all day long, or just a bit earlier to get the Beer && Code party started earlier.  Remember that also, if this Wednesday doesn't work for you.

Time:  5PM - 8PM (or when people leave...)

Date:  Wednesday June 20, 2012

Location:  CoVis CoWorking (map)
