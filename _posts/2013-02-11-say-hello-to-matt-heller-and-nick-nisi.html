Say hi to Matt Heller  (@whatevermatt) and Nick Nisi (@nicknisi)!

By now you've probably met Matt Heller and Nick Nisi.  They are going to be taking over for me to keep the Beer && Code magic alive here in Omaha.  I will still come to Beer && Code, just not every single month.  

A few things let up to this.  First, I've always felt Beer && Code can be more than it is.  I can not do this on my own, heck I've tried and it just didn't go very far.  Things like websites, reaching out to the other locations, and getting word out sooner are all things I wish I could do.  Second, my time this year is going to continue being more and more limited.  I'll keep you all informed with that as things come to pass.

So in order to keep moving forward, Nick and Matt are the main guys here in Omaha.  They will organize each monthly event, get the website from suck to (at least) useable, and reach out to all your Beer && Coders.  I really look forward to their direction.

Finally, I've always said that as long as people show up then we will keep this thing going.  If you like the event, keep showing your pretty faces monthly.  

-Andy
